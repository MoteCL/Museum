our history

What started as a small team of paleontology experts eventually became a huge social project involving lots of enthusiasts and scientists in everything concerning the research of extinct species, including dinosaurs. Our museum underwent a range of considerable changes to become what it is today.

April, 2016
Our museum was founded by a team of dedicated paleonthologists

The concept for the Dino Museum originated in 2001 with the foundation of the American Historic Association. Our main goal at that time was to share more information on paleonthologic researches.

September, 2016
We open new research facility at the Museum

After major renovations were completed at our museum in 2008, we organized a special event for all city residents interested in natural history and introduced our new research facility.

March, 2017
We finish working on new exhibits

After doing lots of research and working on new findings, our paleonthologists, archaeologists, and other scientists finished their work on new exhibits of dinosaurs.
